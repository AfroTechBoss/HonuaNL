Popular questions

How is my impact score calculated?
Your 0–100 score reflects the environmental benefit, scalability, and community engagement of the actions you log. Verified actions count for more — see Impact & verification for the full breakdown.
What are Green Points, Impact Tokens, and HONUA?
Green Points (GP) are earned for logged and verified actions and power levels and streaks. Impact Tokens (IT) are spent in the carbon market. HONUA is the governance token, earned at higher tiers.
How do I reset my password?
On the sign-in screen, choose Forgot password? and we’ll email you a reset link. If it doesn’t arrive, check spam or contact support.
Can I delete a post after publishing?
Yes — open the post, use the “···” menu, and choose Delete. Verified-impact entries tied to the post stay on your ledger unless you remove them separately.
Why aren’t my notifications working?
Check your in-app notification settings and your browser or device permissions. Still stuck? Our support team can take a look.
