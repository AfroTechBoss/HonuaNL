Terms & privacy.

The plain-language version of how Honua works and how we handle your data. The short summaries in green are for orientation; the points below them are what actually applies.

Last updated · May 2026

Terms of service Privacy policy

Terms of service

In short: use Honua honestly, respect other people, keep your impact claims real, and you keep ownership of what you post.

Agreement

By creating an account or using Honua, you agree to these terms and to our community guidelines. If you don’t agree, please don’t use the platform.

Your responsibilities

  • Provide accurate information, including the impact actions you log.
  • Respect other members and follow the community guidelines.
  • Keep your account secure and don’t share your credentials.
  • Don’t misuse the platform — no scraping, fraud, or attempts to game impact scores or rewards.

Content & ownership

You own the content you create. By posting, you grant Honua a licence to display and distribute it within the platform so the network can function. Verified-impact figures may be checked by oracles and third-party auditors before they earn a verified badge.

Rewards

Green Points, Impact Tokens, and HONUA exist to recognise real-world action. They have no guaranteed monetary value, and abuse of the rewards system — including fabricated actions — can result in their removal.

Account termination

  • We may suspend accounts that repeatedly or seriously break these terms.
  • You can appeal any decision by replying to the notification email.
  • You can delete your account at any time; some data may be retained where the law requires it.

Privacy policy

In short: we collect what we need to run Honua and personalise your feed — and you can access, correct, export, or delete your data whenever you like.

What we collect

  • Account information — email, username, and sign-in method.
  • Profile data — bio, interests, and the actions you choose to log.
  • Usage analytics — how you interact with the app, to improve it.
  • Device information — for security and troubleshooting.

How we use it

  • To provide the platform and personalise your feed and project suggestions.
  • To verify impact actions and prevent fraud.
  • To improve features and communicate important updates.

Data sharing

We don’t sell your personal data. We share it only with the third-party services needed to operate Honua (for example, impact verification or payments), when the law requires it, or when you ask us to.

Your rights

  • Access a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correct anything that’s wrong.
  • Export your data in a portable format.
  • Delete your account and associated personal data.

Questions about your data? Email privacy@honua.green or use contact support.
