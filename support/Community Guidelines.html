Community guidelines

A place built on trust.

Honua only works if the impact people share is real and the conversation stays useful. These standards keep it that way — they apply to every post, comment, profile, community, and message.

Last updated · May 2026

Our core values

Respect & inclusivity

Everyone’s welcome, wherever they are on their journey. Disagree with ideas, not people.

Environmental focus

This is a network for fixing the planet. Keep contributions on-topic and constructive.

Constructive dialogue

Ask good questions, share what worked, and help others act — not just argue.

Evidence over hype

Back up impact claims. Verified figures build the trust that sets Honua apart from greenwashing.

What we encourage

  • Supportive, good-faith interactions
  • Sharing accurate, sourced information
  • Respectful disagreement and honest questions
  • Collaborative, practical problem-solving

What’s not allowed

  • Harassment, bullying, or hate toward any person or group
  • Misinformation or fabricated impact claims
  • Spam, scams, and self-promotion without context
  • Off-topic content unrelated to sustainability or community
  • Sharing others’ private information without consent

How we enforce

Most issues are honest mistakes, so we lead with context — not bans. Repeated or serious violations move up the ladder.

A heads-up

We remove the content and explain which guideline it crossed, so you know for next time.

A temporary limit

Repeat issues can pause posting, commenting, or your verified-impact status for a period.

Account suspension

Severe or persistent violations lead to suspension. You can always appeal — see below.

Reporting & appeals

See something off? Use Report a problem on any post, profile, or message — reports are confidential, and our team reviews every one. If you think a decision about your account was a mistake, reply to the notification email to start an appeal.

Need to report something?

Flag a post, person, or safety concern. It only takes a minute and it stays confidential.
